EU Research Funding Network

In this interactive graph, the nodes represent organizations (universities, corporations, research institutes) and arcs represent stable partnerships in the European Commission's Horizon 2020 Framework Programme, 2014-2020 . Stable partnerships are defined by two organisations participating together in two or more projects.

This work was built by Alberto Cottica, Stefano Durì and Federico Piovesan in the context of Spaghetti Open Data. To learn more, see the wiki.
